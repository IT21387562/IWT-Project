The Latest News From The HI Insurance

HI Insurance joins hands with Mobitel to provide exclusive Life Insurance Cover to its customers

Colombo, November 04th, 2020: HI Health Insurance, one of Sri Lanka’s leading health insurance companies, announced that it has entered into an agreement with Mobitel, Sri Lanka’s National Mobile Service Provider, to offer ‘Araksha 365,’ an exclusive health insurance cover to all its customers.

HI Insurance Powers Youth Employability with children

Colombo, August 14th 2020: HI Health Insurance joined hands with children in Sri Lanka to empower youth with essential IT skills under the ‘Shape the World of Tomorrow’ program.

HI PAB Plus Makes Personal Health Insurance More Accessible Offers Enhanced Protection for Policyholders and Their Loved Ones

Continuing to take the benefits of insurance to more people around the island, HI Health Insurance introduced an affordable Personal Health Insurance policy, HI PAB Plus, with a host of additional covers and benefits. The budget-friendly solution is set to offer a higher degree of protection to policyholders and their loved ones in the unfortunate event of a health issue.

HI voted Sri Lanka's most popular health insurer for record 16th year

HI has been voted Sri Lanka’s most popular health insurance company in 2021 and was presented the SLIM Kantar Peoples Award for a record 16th consecutive year at the awards gala hosted by the Sri Lanka Institute of Marketing (SLIM) on 21st March 2022. This remarkable achievement makes HI the only company in its sector to win the coveted SLIM Peoples Award every year since the inception of the awards programme.

HI once again certified a Great Workplace by Great Place to work

HI has been certified a ‘Great Workplace’ in Sri Lanka for a second consecutive year with a five-mark improvement over the overall rating accorded to the Health insurance leader last year, following an independent analysis conducted by Great Place to Work® a global authority on workplace culture.

HI Toastmasters club forges ahead to 8th year

The eighth installation ceremony of HI Insurance’s Toastmasters Club was conducted virtually recently, during which a new executive committee was appointed, new members were welcomed, and the achievers of the past year were honoured.
